Our Story

We saw a gap. We filled it.

Every conversation about AI happened in two worlds. Silicon Valley burned millions on experimental models. Meanwhile, back in India — a school principal, a factory owner, a retail chain manager — all knew AI could transform their operations but had zero idea where to begin.

MarketSarthi was founded to bridge that chasm. We do not sell magic. We sit with you, understand your actual problems, and build AI that solves them using the software you already run, at a price that makes sense for an Indian business.

Today, we have delivered over 200 AI projects across 15 industries. Every single one began with a conversation. The same one we would love to have with you.
