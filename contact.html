FAQ

Questions we often hear

What does an AI project cost?+

Every project is scoped uniquely. Small automation starts around INR 50,000. Comprehensive integrations are quoted after discovery. Transparent, no-obligation estimates during your free consultation. No hidden costs.

How long does implementation take?+

Chatbots: 2–3 weeks. Workflow automation: 4–8 weeks. Predictive systems: 8–16 weeks. Detailed milestone-based timelines provided during discovery.

Do I need to replace my software?+

No. We integrate AI into your existing tools — Tally, Zoho, Salesforce, custom ERPs. Zero rip-and-replace. We work with what you already have and make it smarter.

Is our data secure?+

Absolutely. Enterprise-grade encryption, on-premise deployment options, NDAs as standard. We comply with India's data protection standards. Your data never leaves your control.

Do you train our team?+

Yes. Hands-on workshops, detailed documentation, video tutorials, and ongoing support included. We build your team's AI capability so you become self-sufficient.

What happens after go-live?+

Ongoing support: model monitoring, retraining, performance optimisation, 24x7 helpdesk. We are your long-term AI partner, not a one-project vendor.
